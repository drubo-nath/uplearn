Meet Our Instructor

Jhankar Mahubub

Jhankar Mahbub has been involved in programming and web development for over a decade. After completing his studies at BUET, he did his Masters in Computer Science at North Dakota State University in the United States. He then worked for a long time as a senior web developer at Nielsen Company in the United States. He has written three popular books on simple programming, fun and storytelling. The books are: Programming for the Hubble, the Bull to Boss of Programming, and Fourteen Groups of Programming. He also wrote a guidebook: Recharge your down battery and paracetamol for paramedic life. In his spare time, he made programming and instructional videos for students on the YouTube channel Jhankar Mahbub.

Anisul Islam

He is a passionate computer science teacher. He has been teaching programming languages, web development, and computer science-related subjects to millions of Bangla speaking students worldwide through his YouTube channel for the last eight years. He had the opportunity to teach thousands of Bachelor and Higher secondary students of different institutions in Bangladesh. After completing his masters in Software, Web and Cloud in August 2021, he was spending much time improving my web development skills.

Zulkarine Mahmud

He had born and raised in Bangladesh, currently working as a Software Engineer at Google’s Search team.Before joining Google,he had worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Navis Automotive Systems and also worked with BMW & Daimler AG on their behalf developing Car Navigation Systems.He had finished Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering and CSE (Double major) from Dong-A University, Busan, South Korea under the Korean Government Scholarship.Also, studied in the dept. of Architecture, BUET for 1 year before being selected as a Korean Government Scholar.

Akib Zaman
